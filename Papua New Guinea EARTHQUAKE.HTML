EARTHQUAKES
WHY OCCURS?
Papua New Guinea is located at ring of fire which is a hotspot of seismic activity due to the friction between tectonic plates. There are frequent earthquakes here because of this reason.But no major deaths or damage is recorded.
PAPUA NEW GUINEA EARTHQUAKE IN 2018
The papua New Guinea had a magnitude of 7.5 earthquake that in Papua New Guinea on 26 February 2018.It was damaging damaging mining, gas and power infrastructure, and cutting communications. All the main sources of water were destroyed.
